2. The Google Keep will make sure you'll never forget to pack anything...

Tips
  • Use the checkbox function when you create your packing list
  • Add items to google keep throughout the coming weeks or days before your trip
  • Make sure to add items you need to buy twice, one for the item itself and another stating you need to buy the item
  • Pull out the app while you pack and pack in sequence of list, check the boxes once they have been placed in your luggage.
  • When complete, you can uncheck the boxes and use the same list again before another trip.

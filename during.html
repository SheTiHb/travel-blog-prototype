How to Never Buy and/or Open a Travel Book

Aug 12th, 2014 by Herr. Sheriann

Step1. Go To Hostelworld.com


Step2. Go to Guides and Info and...



Click on "Free Pocket Guides."



Step3. Find Your City:

Browse by continent or if the city is a frequently travelled destination you can find it on list of popular cities on the right hand side.

Step4. Download the guide for your city



It is free. Quick. Portable either by print or mobile device it makes travelling simple and easy. The page with the download link also includes a podcast on something about the city (ex. museums) and a videos with tops on the city. There is also a space to read comments left by travelers to the city. *Note: it opens a seperate tab to find a hostel in that city
